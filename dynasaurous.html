DINOSAURS


DESCRIPTION OF DINOSAURS
Dinosaurs are a diverse group of reptiles of the clade Dinosauria.
They first appeared during the Triassic period, between 243 and 231 million years ago.

ALLOSAURUS: Allosaurus was a typical large theropod, having a massive skull on a short neck, a long tail and reduced forelimbs.
