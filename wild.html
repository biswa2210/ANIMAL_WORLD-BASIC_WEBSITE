WILD WORLD WILD ANIMALS TIGER
The tiger (Panthera tigris) is the largest cat species,
most recognizable for its pattern of dark vertical stripes
on reddish-orange fur with a lighter underside.
The species is classified in the genus Panthera with the lion
, leopard, jaguar, and snow leopard.

Lions have strong, compact bodies and powerful forelegs,
teeth and jaws for pulling down and killing prey.
Their coats are yellow-gold,
and adult males have shaggy manes that range in color from blond to reddish-brown to black.
