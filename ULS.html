Ultrasaurus
Ultrasaurus is a genus of coelurosaurian theropod dinosaur.
This dinosaurus were not eatting other dinosaurus. Ultrasaurus lived throughout what is now western North America,
on what was then an island continent known as Laramidia.,
Ultrasaurus had a much wider range than other Ultrasaurids.
Fossils are found in a variety of rock formations dating to the Maastrichtian age of the upper Cretaceous Period,
680 to 660 million years ago.
